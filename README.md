 # 🌍 EcoSphere – AI Powered ESG Management Platform

> An intelligent ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) management platform that helps organizations monitor sustainability, employee engagement, governance compliance, and carbon emissions in one unified dashboard.

# 📖 Overview

EcoSphere is an AI-powered ESG Management Platform designed to simplify sustainability management for organizations.

The platform enables companies to:

- 🌱 Track Carbon Emissions
- 👨‍💼 Monitor Employee CSR Participation
- 🏆 Gamify Sustainability Initiatives
- 📋 Manage ESG Policies & Compliance
- 📊 Generate ESG Reports
- 🤖 Predict ESG Risks using Machine Learning

Unlike traditional ERP systems where ESG reporting is manual and fragmented, EcoSphere integrates ESG directly into daily business operations.
